# Enable Firebase Authentication - Do This Now! 🔥

You're logged into Firebase CLI with **ashok@jaiswals.live**. Now enable authentication methods.

## 🚀 Quick Method (Recommended)

### Open Firebase Console and Enable Auth Providers:

**Direct Link:** https://console.firebase.google.com/project/homemaid-f7a6e/authentication/providers

### 1. Enable Email/Password (Required)
- Click on "Email/Password" row
- Toggle **Enable** to ON
- Click **Save**

### 2. Enable Google (Required)
- Click on "Google" row
- Toggle **Enable** to ON
- **Project support email:** ashok@jaiswals.live
- Click **Save**

### 3. Enable Apple (Optional - for later)
- Click on "Apple" row
- Toggle **Enable** to ON
- Requires Apple Developer credentials
- Can skip for now and enable later

---

## ✅ After Enabling

Once you've enabled Email/Password and Google:

1. **Refresh your browser** at http://localhost:3000
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Log in"**
3. Try signing up with email
4. Try Google Sign-In

The `auth/operation-not-allowed` error will be gone! ✨
